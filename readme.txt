Simple and flexible online appointments for your clients. Add a BUK.app apointment shortcode to your WordPress site.

== Installation ==

* Go to any post/page and include a "buk" shortcode with a single slug parameter refering to your BUK.app account (e.g., for https://example.buk.app use [buk slug="example"]).

== Description ==
Online booking software for your business. With your own-branded booking page your clients can easily see if you're available and make appointments on the fly. Access your online calendar, manage your appointments and notify your clients with unlimited automatic reminders via SMS and email. With the plugin you can add a buk.app shortcode to your WordPress site.

Getting started:

1. Create your free account at [BUK.app](https://buk.app/) 
2. Add your "buk" shortcode to any page on your site.
3. You're done :)


== Frequently Asked Questions ==
=  What is BUK for? =
BUK allows your customers to book appointments anywhere at anytime by themselves, without phoning, emailing or texting you.

= Is BUK only for barbershops?  =
Absolutely not! BUK is made for any business that need to schedule appointments. It's used everyday by beauty salons, SPAs, personal trainers, physiotherapist, and many more professionals.

= How do I install BUK? =
There is nothing to install. BUK uses the latest web technologies so your customers can access it on any device (mobile phones included).
